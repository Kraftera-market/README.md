## Problem Statement

The global digital content market is a multi-trillion-dollar industry, yet creators capture only a fraction of the value they generate.

### 1. Centralization & Gatekeeping

Traditional digital marketplaces operate as centralized intermediaries that dictate pricing, content policies, and payment schedules.

**Impact:** Loss of creative freedom and business continuity risk.

### 2. Excessive Fees & Revenue Leakage

Platforms routinely charge commissions of **30-70%** on every sale.

**Impact:** Structural inequity, especially for independent creators.

### 3. Lack of Provenance & Royalty Enforcement

Once a digital file is sold, it can be duplicated and redistributed without compensation.

**Impact:** Creators cannot build sustainable income.

### 4. Poor User Experience & Fragmented Liquidity

Complex wallet setups, high gas fees, and fragmented liquidity across chains.

**Impact:** High barrier to entry for mainstream users.
